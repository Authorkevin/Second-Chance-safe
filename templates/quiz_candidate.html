Consider the following questions when evaluating candidates for your community:

1. What is the most important issue facing your community right now?

2. How do you want your future local leader to support the community?

3. What kind of leadership style do you believe will best serve your community?

4. How important is transparency and honesty in a local leader for you?

5. Which of the following should a leader focus on to ensure community well-being?

6. How do you prefer a local leader to involve you in decision-making?

7. What experience do you think is most important for a candidate to have?

8. How important is it for a leader to be connected to other communities or regions?

9. What is your preferred way of getting updates from a local leader?

10. Lastly, what do you believe is the most important quality for a leader to have?

This quiz helps you reflect on the qualities and values you prioritize in a community leader. Consider these factors when evaluating candidates in your local elections.
